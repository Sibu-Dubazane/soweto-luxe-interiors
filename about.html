Who We Are

Soweto Luxe Interiors is on a mission to define living spaces with custom-built units & solutions that blend style, functionality, and lasting quality. We believe every home deserves a personalised design that works in harmony with your lifestyle.

Our Journey

Quiet Start, Bold Vision

It started with two people from different worlds, tech and interior solutions. Brought together by a shared passion for helping others. With a keen eye for design and a vision to improve everyday spaces and experiences, they turned their skills into a purpose-driven venture.

Word Spreads Fast

Long before officially launching, we had already been offering our services informally—driven by passion and purpose. As word spread, demand grew organically. We've built a loyal local client base through custom projects and trusted referrals.

2024 - A Real Business

We formalised our team, launched our brand, and expanded into full-house custom interior solutions — including kitchens, bedrooms, TV walls, electrical, plumbing, painting, tiling, and more.
